Hi, I'm Matthew! A little over one year ago, I started Wholesome Games to bring attention to an emerging genre of games less concerned with how you play (shooter, platformer, etc.) and more concerned with how they made you feel. Since then Wholesome Games has made over 35,000 friends.

Later this spring we’ll host our first Wholesome Direct, an online video showcase meant to bring further attention to some of the most promising wholesome games. Our hope is that this showcase brings joy to both the developers and fans involved. Please look forward to exclusive footage and announcements from games like Ooblets, Spiritfarer, SkateBIRD, Little Witch in the Woods, Garden Story, Chicory, Rainy Season, and many more!

founders

Matthew Taylor is the name behind the Twitter, where he loves to share wonderful titles he's discovered. It's really quite special to find something that resonates with so many, bringing us a little closer and a little happier with every tweet. He currently works as the creative director for Rolling Hills: Make Sushi, Make Friends. In his spare time, Matthew has contributed to Wireframe Magazine and produced music for film and television.
